FAQ

Short answers before a user decides whether $19 is worth it.

Does this upload my form data?

No. LeadFill stores one profile locally in Chrome storage and only fills the active tab after a user click.

Is this a subscription?

$19 lifetime unlocks the current LeadFill lifetime plan. It is a one-time purchase, not a recurring subscription.

What happens after the 10 free fills?

LeadFill keeps the product usable for evaluation first. After the free fills are used, the extension asks you to unlock Lifetime or refresh an existing membership.

Does it work on every form?

It is built for common text, email, phone, textarea, and select fields. It skips readonly and disabled fields, and it avoids overwriting existing values by default.

How do I restore a purchase?

Sign in with the same email through OTP, then use Refresh membership. LeadFill reads the active entitlement from the pay site.

When does Lifetime unlock after payment?

Only after the payment webhook updates your entitlement. The success page does not unlock anything locally.

試してみますか？

Use the free fills first. Unlock Lifetime when LeadFill becomes part of your daily flow.
