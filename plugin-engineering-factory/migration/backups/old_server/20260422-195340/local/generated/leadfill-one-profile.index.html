FAQ

Commercial clarity matters as much as clean UI.

Does this upload my form data?

No. LeadFill stores one profile locally in Chrome storage and only fills the active tab after a user click.

Do I need an account to use the free tier?

No account is required for the free local workflow. HWH email login only matters for future paid membership checks.

When does Pro unlock after payment?

Only after HWH or Waffo webhook processing updates the entitlement. A success page alone does not unlock the extension.

Can I buy it right now?

Not yet. The public checkout remains disabled until product key, plan, SMTP, and webhook validation are complete.

Why does the page mention email login availability?

SMTP delivery is still a known blocker. The UI must stay honest and show login as unavailable or coming soon until OTP is verified end-to-end.

What does the lifetime license include?

Unlimited fills for the current major version, plus the local profile workflow and advanced field support already shown in the screenshots.
