# Manual Evidence Examples

These examples show the difference between weak, medium, strong, and counter-signal manual evidence. Do not store private customer data, secrets, or confidential notes in these artifacts.

## Weak Evidence

`self_observation`

> "我自己觉得客服可能需要这个。"

Why it is weak:

- It is internal speculation only.
- It does not prove repeated pain.
- It cannot promote a candidate by itself.

## Medium Evidence

`internal_workflow`

> "团队 QA 每次报 bug 都要复制 URL 和浏览器版本。"

Why it is medium:

- It names a concrete repetitive workflow.
- It still needs a second independent source.
- It does not yet prove local-only or text-first preference.

## Strong Evidence

`customer_support`

> "3 位 support agent 都说他们每天多次手工复制 URL、浏览器版本和复现步骤到 Zendesk/Jira，并且不想上传页面内容。"

Why it is strong:

- Multiple users report the same pain.
- The handoff fields are explicit.
- It includes a local-only / no-upload preference.
- It is close to install intent for the narrowed wedge.

## Counter-Signal

`user_interview`

> "用户说他们只想要截图/视频，不需要文本。"

Why it matters:

- This is valid evidence against a text-first wedge.
- Enough counter-signal should move the candidate to `skip`.
