# SRT to Markdown Article Product Brief

## Single Purpose

Convert a user-selected `.srt` subtitle file into a clean Markdown article draft locally in the browser.

## Target Users

- Video note takers
- Course organizers
- Podcast and interview editors
- Obsidian / Notion knowledge-base users
- Self-media operators who turn captions into article drafts

## MVP

- Upload one `.srt` file
- Optionally attach one video file for local preview and filename context only
- Parse subtitles
- Clean timestamps and line breaks
- Merge captions into readable paragraphs
- Render Markdown article, Obsidian note, or timestamped notes
- Copy Markdown
- Download `.md`
- Track 10 free conversions locally
- Show upgrade button after free quota
- Bilingual English / Chinese UI

## Non-Goals

- No ASR
- No video transcoding
- No cloud upload
- No AI rewriting
- No host permissions
- No automatic publishing
