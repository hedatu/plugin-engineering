# Transcript to Markdown Studio Product Brief

## Single Purpose

Convert local transcript files (`.srt`, `.vtt`, `.txt`) into clean Markdown articles, notes, outlines, or meeting minutes in the browser.

## Target Users

- Video note takers
- Course organizers
- Podcast and interview editors
- Obsidian / Notion knowledge-base users
- Self-media operators who turn captions or transcripts into article drafts
- Meeting operators who export TXT transcripts and need structured notes

## v0.2.0 Scope

- Upload one or more `.srt`, `.vtt`, or `.txt` transcript files
- Optionally attach one video file for local preview and filename context only
- Parse timed subtitle transcripts and plain text transcripts
- Merge multiple files into one Markdown document
- Render Markdown article, Obsidian note, timestamped notes, blog outline, or meeting minutes
- Copy Markdown
- Download `.md`
- Track 10 free conversions locally
- Show upgrade button after free quota
- Bilingual English / Chinese UI

## Non-Goals

- No ASR
- No video transcoding
- No cloud upload
- No AI rewriting
- No image OCR in v0.2.0
- No host permissions
- No automatic Chrome upload or publish
