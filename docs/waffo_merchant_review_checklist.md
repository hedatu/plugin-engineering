Product: LeadFill One Profile

## Product clarity

- Product purpose is clear on the homepage and product page.
- The site reads like a real plugin product site, not a test shell.
- The product page explains the real use case: filling repetitive lead forms from one local profile.
- The site does not mention `chatgpt2obsidian`.

## Pricing clarity

- The price is clearly shown as `$19 one-time`.
- The free plan is clearly shown as `10 free fills`.
- The difference between free and paid access is clear.
- The site does not imply subscription billing when the offer is lifetime unlock.
- The site does not falsely imply that production payment is already enabled beyond the current mode.

## Merchant trust and policy pages

- `Refund` page exists and is written as a real policy page.
- `Privacy` page exists and clearly explains the local-only data boundary.
- `Terms` page exists and clearly explains free and paid access, license scope, and contact.
- Support email is visible in legal pages.

## Checkout and entitlement clarity

- Product pricing page explains that checkout starts on the site and completes on a hosted payment page.
- The site explains that membership appears after backend confirmation.
- The site states that the success page does not unlock Pro locally.
- The site states that the extension or account page reads refreshed entitlement after payment.

## Placeholder and quality checks

- No placeholder sections remain on the core commercial pages.
- No fake or unimplemented features are advertised.
- Success and cancel pages are minimal, clear, and not misleading.
- Account page is operational but not presented as a debug panel.
